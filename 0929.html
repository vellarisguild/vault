Discussion Deep Dive

Tool & Community Strategy Marketing Plan Review Conference & Partnerships Feedback Session

The team aims to use free tools to generate leads. The finalized strategy is to offer one premium tool for free each week on a rotating basis. Access will be granted via a chatbot on direct message, which will capture the user's business email and a qualifying question. The tool link will be delivered automatically and will expire after seven days. This aligns with core values of providing value, maintaining exclusivity, and creating a sustainable conversion engine.

Led by Tong, the plan involves an initial 10,000 baht budget each for Facebook and Instagram ads. Content will be a mix of video (30-60s, costing 300-350 baht each) and banners to test ROI. Ads will lead directly to the sales page. The strategy also includes continuous website UI/UX improvements and audience engagement across platforms like Facebook groups, LINE, and TikTok, supplemented by live Q&A sessions.

A key partnership opportunity was discussed. Tong is tasked with following up with 'Nobita' to explore potential speaking engagements and other collaboration opportunities. This is part of the broader strategy to expand reach and authority in the industry.

A general feedback session was held. The discussion included fixing website issues, such as ensuring all links are clickable and addressing font and style discrepancies on mobile. The overall meeting concluded with a positive outlook on the team's progress and future prospects.
